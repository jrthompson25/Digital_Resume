Professional Advancement

Post-College Work

Since earning my degree, I have committed myself to mastery of languages taught while in college, but also others that were not. Shown below are my certificates of completion in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery is soon to follow. These certificates were obtained through completing two books in the A Smarter Way to Learn series, which are used as part of the course material for DevMountain - a developer bootcamp. One covers HTML & CSS and the other covers JavaScript, as you can see on the certificates themselves. These books are written in a way that increases the reader's retention of the material tremendously.

Each chapter is a brief, but informative, explanation of each coding concept. After the reader completes each chapter, there are twenty rigorous exercises for the concept the chapter covers, totaling to 1780 exercises upon completion of each book. However, this number does not include repeating exercises for the sake of reviewing more complicated concepts, so the number of exercises I have completed is actually more. These books are not only an excellent way to learn a language, but are also a wonderful source of reference while in the middle of developing or to review. There are several languages the writer covers and to see which those are as well as reviews from more developers, you can visit his website.
