Introduction

Hello, My name is Jason Thompson. I am the creator of this website. Here you will be introduced to my education and professional development. The sole purpose of this website is to showcase my skills as a Front-End Developer utilizing languages such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and jQuery. It has been created strictly by coding from scratch; there has been no use of a CMS such as WordPress, Drupal, or Joomla and there were no themes downloaded online or anything such as that.

While it was created in Dreamweaver for part of the time, no drag-and-drop design was used. In short, I have only used coding concepts I have learned through attaining my degree and my self-taught study of these languages, nothing more. Please take a few moments to browse and observe by using the navigation bar which reveals itself when clicking on the menu bars in the top-left corner. If you wish to contact me, you can do so by clicking the buttons in the top-right corner. Thank you for visiting!
